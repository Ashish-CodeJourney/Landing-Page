About Vyapar Sahayak

Vyapar Sahayak is a cross-platform application designed to empower micro and small enterprises (MSEs) by acting as a mediator between sellers and buyers. Our mission is to simplify business interactions and help MSEs thrive in the digital marketplace.

  • Business-to-Business (B2B) Transactions: Vyapar Sahayak currently supports B2B transactions, and it has the potential to expand its functionality to enable Business-to-Customer (B2C) interaction in the future.

  • Direct Customer-Seller Engagement: Our application empowers customers to engage directly with sellers, fostering more personalized and interactive interactions.

  • Market Expansion: Micro-businesses can expand their reach, enhance sales and marketing efforts, and attract a larger customer base to boost revenue.

  • New Opportunities: Vyapar Sahayak is designed to facilitate market expansion for businesses, enabling them to enter new opportunities and customer segments.

Join us on our mission to boost local economies, promote self-reliance, and empower Swadeshi businesses.
